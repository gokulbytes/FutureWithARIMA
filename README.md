# FutureWithARIMA

As a Data Science Intern at Upskill Campus, I worked on projects that combined technical depth with real-world impact—focusing on predictive modeling, data visualization, and storytelling with data.

One of my key contributions was developing an Energy Demand Forecasting model using the ARIMA time series approach. I handled the entire workflow: preparing and cleaning datasets, running stationarity checks, analyzing autocorrelation patterns, and tuning parameters to optimize accuracy. The model delivered reliable demand predictions, demonstrating how data science can drive resource optimization, cost reduction, and sustainable energy planning. This project deepened my expertise in time series forecasting while showing the practical value of applying data-driven insights to industry challenges.

I also designed and deployed interactive dashboards and reporting solutions using Power BI, Tableau, and Python visualization libraries. These dashboards transformed raw data into clear, actionable insights that supported real-time monitoring and faster decision-making. My focus was usability and clarity—ensuring that both technical and non-technical stakeholders could quickly grasp trends and make informed choices.

This internship strengthened my technical skills in Python, SQL, data preprocessing, feature engineering, and visualization, while also sharpening my ability to communicate insights effectively and collaborate with cross-functional teams.

Overall, my experience at Upskill Campus was about more than coding—it was about creating solutions that bridge the gap between data and strategy, delivering tangible value by forecasting the future and illuminating the present.
